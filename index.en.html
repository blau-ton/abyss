What Abyss does

Log in 60 s

Depth, duration, visibility, buddy, notes — in one compact entry form.

Spots library

Map, your own photos, ratings, favorites. Synced with your dive log.

Import & export

CSV, UDDF, Suunto DM5, Garmin FIT — you keep your data in open formats.

Live weather

Current air temperature at your last spot via Apple WeatherKit. Anonymized.

QR sharing

Share dives between buddies via QR code. Device to device, no server.

Stats

Total depth, averages, water temperatures, awards — all at a glance.

Privacy. Abyss collects no data. Everything you log stays on your iPhone. The only external service is Apple WeatherKit for the air-temperature display — operated anonymously by Apple.
